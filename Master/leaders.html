Our Team Leaders

Meet the exceptional players who have shaped our basketball legacy. Their leadership, skill, and dedication have inspired teammates and brought numerous victories to Nanyang Polytechnic’s basketball program.

This is LeBron Tan, captain of the NYP Titans from 2022–2024. Known for his leadership under pressure, he led the team to three consecutive POL-ITE Championships. His drive, work ethic, and ability to rally the team made him the heartbeat of our dynasty run.
These leaders have set the standard for teamwork, resilience, and sportsmanship. Their influence extends beyond the court, fostering a culture of unity and excellence within the team.

About Our Basketball Team

Where Passion Meets Purpose

At Nanyang Polytechnic’s Basketball CCA, we believe true success is built on more than just winning games. Our players train hard, compete fiercely, and most importantly, lift each other up — on and off the court.

Through dedication, resilience, and unity, our team has become a symbol of excellence and sportsmanship. Every practice, every game, and every challenge strengthens our bond and sharpens our skills.

Our Core Values:

  • Teamwork that drives success
  • Resilience in the face of challenges
  • Leadership that inspires others
  • Commitment to sportsmanship

Join us, and be part of a legacy where every player contributes to something greater — a team that grows together and inspires beyond the court.
